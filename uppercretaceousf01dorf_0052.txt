In the discussion of most of the species in the Medicine Bow floras, suggestions
have been made, where possible, regarding what appear to be the more likely
family and generic relationships. In several new species, however, pending more
conclusive evidence, the leaves have been referred to the form genus Phyllius and
the seeds to Carpites. Systematic descriptions of previously described species have
been amended and new illustrations added where better material has been obtained.

and complete bibliographic references of synonymous forms are given under each
species in the systematic descriptions.
